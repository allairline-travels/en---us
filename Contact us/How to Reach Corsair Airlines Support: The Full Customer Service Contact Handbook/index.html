📞 Corsair Airlines Customer Service Full Guide

Need assistance from Corsair Airlines? Here's your complete customer service guide with regional contact details, hours of operation, and ways to get help quickly. 🌍

📱 Phone Support

Corsair offers several phone support options based on your location:

  • 🇫🇷 From France: Dial 3917 (charged at €0.35/min from a landline)
  • 🌐 International Calls: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇺🇸 North America: +1 888-657-8380

🏢 Local Office Support

You can also contact Corsair's regional offices directly:

  • 📍 New York: +1 888-657-8380
  • 📍 Los Angeles: +1 888-657-8380

✉️ Email Support

Prefer to write? Send your inquiry to corsair.res@apg-ga.ca. Be sure to include:

  • 🧑‍💼 Your full name
  • 📄 Booking reference
  • ✈️ Flight details
  • 📝 Clear description of your issue

🕒 Hours of Operation

France: Daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM (local time)

North America: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM (local office time zones)

🛬 Airport Assistance

If you're at the airport and need help with flight changes, check-in, baggage issues, or cancellations, visit the Corsair customer service desk.

✅ Tip: Have your booking reference and valid ID ready to speed up service.
